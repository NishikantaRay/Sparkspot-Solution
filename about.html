Our Journey

It was a purpose filled with passion. The purpose is to do something for the people around us. Off course using the knowledge and experience gained in so many years. Knowledge, acquired in a hard way and experience, endured us to understand the dynamics of the world order. Failing and rising up, again failing and bouncing back more intelligently were the part of life. But effort was to make things that help the business, the people engaged in the business, the state and the nation, eventually. There was always an inner calling which constantly prompts; every action of ours should empower others with skill, freedom, new ideas, innovation and sustainability.

We think, that gave birth to sparkspot with a belief that every person in the earth has potential and a dream. Identifying the potential, helping him know about that, further sharpening it and enabling him to use that for the good of him and others, is the sole idea and the beginning of the story of sparkspot. The search is relentless to “spot the spark within” many a times latent but most of the time underutilized.

This was all about the idea. Idea unless converted into action is meaningless. We had to get the people together who match the same wavelength and selfless goal. We need also to decide the areas of application and our prospective stakeholders. It took time. But here we are. A team with 200 hundred years of industry experience in the matters related to people, engineering, technology, projects, commodities and more importantly sustainability.

We strongly believe what Steve Jobs had said once; “we are here to make a dent in the universe, otherwise why even be here”. We get challenged and equally excited and energized every day morning we wake and set our foot to create a mark somewhere “a dent in precise” to be better than yesterday.

VISION
