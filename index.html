Words of Wisdom

What is observed in a person is 10% of his personality. Many a time we get hooked with the 10% and form our opinion about the person. Irony is the person himself doesn’t know what lies within him. This is a big gap. In fact, we are much more than what is manifested in our action. The idea of Sparkspot came when we tried to understand this underneath potential of individuals, organizations and entities and started probing them to search, find and foster this infinite potential for their good and for the good of others.

In Sparkspot we design intelligent interventions keeping technology at the forefront develop training modules, take up mentorship, build people strategy, promote productivity and push performance agenda in all aspects operation, for our clients. But above all we are conscious of one thing; our action must make others better than what they were before.

“That is the philosophy of our pursuance. Our aim is simple. That is to “enable” and not “teach”.”

Sanjay Pattnaik

Co-founder & CEO

Words of Wisdom

The best thing that could have happened to me is possibly “SparkSpot”. The day begins with a joy of making a positive impact on somebody’s life and his future through our action. In my opinion the joy of giving truly mean is empowering people, organizations and individuals, with the required knowledge and skill and allow them to fight their own battle rightfully and confidently. In SparksSpot we strongly believe in three things whether it is an organization or an individual.

● Everyone in this earth has a dream
● And they have the potential to realize that dream
● It takes time, because, they operate at 40% of their capacity, unaware of their possessed potential

SparkSpot as a team is relentlessly working on making entities understand their inherent capacity and the ways to leverage that to their advantage. We work in the areas of people, organization and business with a simple philosophy in mind; growth is continuous and change is imminent .

J K Mohanty
